about us:

When you buy a new phone, or a new laptop, what do you do with the old one? Put it in the drawer and forget about it? Thought so, and we don’t blame you at all either. There is a lot of admin and time that goes into selling something, it especially seems pointless if its old and not worth a whole lot.

That’s why get rid is around. We are here to take that old unwanted tech of yours and sell it for you, while you can comfortably sit back and let us make the money for you!

At get rid, we take convenience seriously. We want the process of getting rid of things as easy as possible for you, so it can be passed on just as quickly. This way the items stay in their lifecycle and people can enjoy them for the entirety of their life.

Our mission is to create a seamless and convenient experience from door to door, ensuring that each and every piece of tech is given a home until the end of its life cycle.
