pricing:

how it works:

1. We come to your door to collect your items.

2. We will also ask you to provide your bank number for later on.

3. Once we have successfully sold ALL your items we will send 30% of the total sales money back to you via the bank account number you provided when we collected your items.

4. We will also provide evidence of the sale and the sale price.

Broken/ non-repairable / non-sellable goods:

Anything that is no longer of value to anyone and cannot be sold such as highly internally/externally damaged items, non-repairable items etc will go to an e-waste site where it will be disposed of correctly or recycled for other uses. This means that you will NOT receive any money from anything that cannot be sold and in turn ends up in e-waste.
